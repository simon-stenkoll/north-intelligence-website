05

Voice

Plainspoken. Technical when it earns the space. Never posture, never sell.

Do
  • Say what the deliverable is.
  • Name the tools when it matters (dbt, Snowflake, Airflow).
  • Talk about tradeoffs, not features.
  • Show the work — code snippets, DAGs, small numbers.
Don't
  • "Unlock", "empower", "leverage", "synergy".
  • Stock photos of laptops and dashboards.
  • Overpromise on ML.
  • Emoji in professional writing.
